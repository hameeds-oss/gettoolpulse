What Is Midjourney?

Midjourney is a text-to-image AI that turns written prompts into highly detailed, often stunning images. It's known for a distinctive, polished aesthetic that tends to look more "artistic" out of the box than competitors like DALL-E or Stable Diffusion.

Who Should Use It?

Midjourney is ideal if image quality is your top priority and you're willing to spend a little time learning prompts. Designers, marketers, and content creators get the most out of it. If you need precise control or want to run it locally and free, Stable Diffusion may suit you better.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free version of Midjourney?
Midjourney removed its free trial due to high demand. You'll need at least the $10/month Basic plan to generate images.
Can I use Midjourney images commercially?
Yes, paid subscribers can use generated images commercially, though specific terms apply for large companies. Always check the current license terms.
Is Midjourney better than DALL-E?
For pure artistic quality, most users prefer Midjourney. DALL-E is easier for beginners and better at following literal instructions. It depends on your use case.
