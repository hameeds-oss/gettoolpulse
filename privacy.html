Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what information ToolPulse ("we", "us") collects when you visit gettoolpulse.com, how we use it, and the choices you have. We've tried to write it in plain language.

Information we collect

We collect very little directly. Specifically:

  • Email address — only if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.
  • Usage data — basic, anonymous analytics such as which pages are visited, your approximate region, browser type, and how you arrived at the site. This helps us understand what's useful and improve it.
  • Messages you send us — if you email us, we keep that correspondence to reply to you.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored in your browser. We and our partners use them to understand site usage and, where applicable, to serve advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some parts of the site may work less smoothly.

Advertising and third parties

We may use third-party advertising companies, including Google, to serve ads. These companies may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to our site and others.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies via aboutads.info.
  • For more on how Google handles data, see Google's policy.

Analytics

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymous, aggregated statistics about site traffic. This data isn't used to personally identify you.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click them, the destination site may set its own cookies to track the referral. This is what allows us to earn a commission. See our affiliate disclosure for details.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, or to opt out of certain processing. To exercise any of these, just email us and we'll help.

Children

This site is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date at the top.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or this policy? Email hello@gettoolpulse.com.
